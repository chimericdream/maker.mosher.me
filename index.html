Project Ideas

Here are some other project ideas to get you started.

  • Mute your phone when you arrive at work.
  • Automatically turn on your porch light when it is dusk.
  • Text yourself a todo item and have it sent to your todo list manager.
  • Feed your pet with a button press.
  • Find your keys using SmartThings Presence Sensor.
  • Alert your phone when your front door opens and nobody is home.
  • Control your thermostat from your watch or phone.
  • Automatically track the mileage of your car.
  • Turn on/off your porch light from your phone.
  • Add a task to your todo list to bring an umbrella if it is supposed to rain.
  • Save articles to Evernote from Feedly for free with IFTTT
  • Unmute your phone when you get two calls from the same number within 2 minutes. "It must be an emergency".
  • Get an alert when the International Space Station flies overhead.
  • Automatically log check engine light diagnostics to evernote as soon as the light comes on.
